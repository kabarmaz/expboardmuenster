5) Challenges & Limitations 

•  OSM Completeness & Dating: Edit latency and inconsistent coverage; rely on 

presence/absence between snapshots with spatial tolerance 

• 

Imagery Availability & Licensing: DOP coverage by flight year; confirm licensing 

for redistribution 

•  Temporal Misalignment: OSM edit timestamps vs orthophoto capture dates; 

adopt buffers and document uncertainty 

•  Model Bias & Generalization: DL underperformance in dense/occluded/informal 

areas; mitigate with local fine-tuning and spatial CV 

•  Environmental Estimation: Tree counts from canopy area depend on 

species/crown sizes; report ranges and perform sensitivity analyses 

•  Compute & Storage: DL training and tiling require GPU and disk; batch by AOI 

and cache tiles; consider cloud if needed
