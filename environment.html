Methodology

Canopy loss was estimated using a model-based approach combining building data with vegetation coverage estimates:

Canopy_Loss = Buildings × Footprint × Dev_Multiplier × Canopy_Coverage(zone)

Where:
• Footprint = 150 m² (average building)
• Dev_Multiplier = 1.8× (roads, parking, setbacks)
• Canopy_Coverage = 15-55% (varies by zone)

CO2 sequestration was calculated using established urban forestry metrics:

Trees = Canopy_Area × 80 trees/ha
CO2 = Trees × 22 kg/tree/year
